Vegan Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles (whole wheat or gluten-free, as per preference)
  • 2 cups of tomato sauce
  • 1 cup of vegan ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup of spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup of mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup of zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup of bell peppers, diced
  • 1 cup of vegan mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for greasing

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a pan, sauté the minced garlic in olive oil until fragrant. Add the mushrooms, zucchini, and bell peppers. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Add the chopped spinach to the pan and cook until wilted. Season with salt, pepper, dried oregano, and dried basil. Mix well.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine the vegan ricotta cheese with a pinch of salt.
  6. Grease a baking dish with olive oil. Place a layer of lasagna noodles at the bottom.
  7. Spread a layer of tomato sauce over the noodles, followed by a layer of the sautéed vegetable mixture.
  8. Add dollops of vegan ricotta cheese on top of the vegetables.
  9. Repeat the layers until you run out of ingredients, finishing with a layer of tomato sauce on top.
  10. Sprinkle vegan mozzarella cheese over the final layer.
  11. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Then, uncover and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the top is golden and bubbly.
  12. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
