Vegan Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza crust (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup of tomato sauce
  • 1 cup of vegan mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup of cherry tomatoes, sliced
  • 1/2 cup of red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup of bell peppers, sliced
  • 1/4 cup of black olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to the temperature recommended for your pizza crust (usually around 425°F or 220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza crust on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  3. Spread an even layer of tomato sauce over the crust, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle vegan mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  5. Place cherry tomatoes, red onion, bell peppers, and black olives on top of the cheese.
  6. Drizzle olive oil over the pizza and sprinkle dried oregano. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for the time specified for your pizza crust or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  9. Top the pizza with fresh basil leaves before serving.
  10. Slice and enjoy your delicious vegan pizza!

Enjoy
