Vegan Tiramisu

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1/4 cup coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 1 package (12-14 oz) of firm silken tofu
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup or agave nectar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup plant-based milk (e.g., almond, soy, or oat milk)
  • 24-30 vegan ladyfinger cookies
  • Cocoa powder for dusting
  • Vegan chocolate shavings (optional)

Instructions

  1. Brew a cup of strong coffee and let it cool. If desired, add coffee liqueur to the cooled coffee.
  2. In a blender, combine silken tofu, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and plant-based milk. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Dip each ladyfinger into the cooled coffee mixture, ensuring they are moistened but not overly soaked.
  4. Arrange a layer of dipped ladyfingers in the bottom of a serving dish or individual glasses.
  5. Spread a layer of the tofu mixture over the ladyfingers.
  6. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of the tofu mixture on top.
  7. Refrigerate the tiramisu for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow it to set.
  8. Before serving, dust the top with cocoa powder and garnish with vegan chocolate shavings if desired.
  9. Serve chilled and enjoy your delightful vegan tiramisu!
